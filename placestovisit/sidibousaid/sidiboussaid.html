INFORMATION:...............................

Sidi Bou Saïd, a gem perched on the heights of Tunis, captivates with its white houses with blue shutters, flower-lined alleys, and breathtaking views of the sea. This village of artists, both inspiring and romantic, is a unique blend of tradition, history, and Mediterranean beauty. Enjoy a pine nut tea overlooking the bay, stroll through art galleries, and let yourself be enchanted by the bohemian and peaceful atmosphere. In Sidi Bou Saïd, every corner is a postcard, every moment a timeless escape.
