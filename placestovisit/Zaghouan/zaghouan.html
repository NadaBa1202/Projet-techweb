Zaghouane

INFORMATION:...............................

Zaghouan is a picturesque town located in northern Tunisia, nestled at the foot of the Atlas Mountains. Known for its lush landscapes and rich history, Zaghouan is most famous for its ancient Roman water temple, the Temple of the Water, which once supplied water to the city of Carthage through a sophisticated aqueduct system. The town is also a gateway to the Djebel Zaghouan mountain, a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers. With its blend of natural beauty, historical sites, and traditional architecture, Zaghouan offers visitors a unique and tranquil experience away from the busier coastal cities.
