Monastir

INFORMATION:...............................

Monastir is a historic coastal city in central-eastern Tunisia, located on a peninsula in the Gulf of Hammamet. Approximately 162 kilometers south of Tunis, it serves as the capital of the Monastir Governorate .

Monastir is renowned for its rich cultural tapestry. The Great Mosque, dating back to the 9th century, exemplifies Islamic architecture. The Medina, with its narrow alleys and bustling souks, offers a glimpse into traditional Tunisian life. The Museum of Folk Arts and Traditions showcases local crafts, including intricately embroidered garments .
